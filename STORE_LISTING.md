## Full description

**Turn yard sales, thrift finds, and clearance racks into real profit.**

Score Flipp is the fastest way to know if an item is worth flipping. Snap a photo (or scan a barcode) and our AI tells you in seconds:

✓ **What it is** — auto-identified item, brand, and category
✓ **What it's worth** — realistic resale price range
✓ **Your profit** — fees, shipping, and net profit calculated for you
✓ **Demand** — High / Medium / Low so you don't sit on dead stock
✓ **Best marketplace** — eBay, Mercari, Poshmark, StockX, Depop, Facebook — we pick the right one

**Built for resellers, by resellers.**

• Save scans and build a watchlist
• Compare two items side-by-side
• Track real profit with "Mark as sold"
• See trending categories before they peak
• Export your inventory to CSV

**Free to try.** 5 scans every month, forever. Upgrade to **Pro** for unlimited scans, batch mode, and price-drop alerts.

Whether you're a full-time reseller or just clearing out the garage — stop guessing, start flipping.
